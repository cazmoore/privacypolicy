Clip Plan – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 Nov 2025

Clip Plan (“the Extension”) is a browser extension that lets you save webpages to your Trello boards quickly and securely. We prioritize your privacy, collect only the data required for core functionality, and never sell or share your information.

1. Information We Collect

Data you provide

Clip Plan collects the minimum data necessary to perform its features, including:

  • Trello board and list selections (board IDs, list IDs)
  • Optional fields you enter, such as card title overrides, descriptions, or notes
  • Extension settings (default board/list, theme preference, AI summary toggle)

Automatically collected metadata

When clipping a webpage, the extension may gather:

  • Page title
  • Page URL
  • Favicon or preview image
  • Meta description or available summary data

AI notes (optional)

If enabled, page content snippets may be sent to your configured AI provider (such as OpenAI or a local API endpoint) solely to generate summaries.

Note: Clip Plan does not control how your chosen AI provider handles data. Please review your provider’s privacy policy for details on logging or model training.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

Clip Plan stores data locally on your device only:

  • Chrome Extension Storage – for settings, cached OAuth tokens, and user preferences
  • IndexedDB – for offline or queued clips and metadata cache

No data is transmitted to any Clip Plan server. All Trello operations occur directly between your browser and Trello’s API under your explicit authorization.

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to:

  • Authenticate your Trello account through Trello’s OAuth2 flow
  • Create Trello cards when you clip a webpage
  • Display your boards, lists, and recent selections in the UI
  • Provide optional AI-generated summaries or notes (if enabled)
  • Apply your user-controlled settings and preferences

We do not use your data for analytics, profiling, advertising, or tracking.

4. Data Sharing and Selling

Clip Plan does not:

  • Sell your data
  • Share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising
  • Use third-party analytics within the extension
  • Track your activity across websites

The only external service used is Trello’s API, accessed with your permission and solely to create or update Trello cards on your behalf.

5. Trello Authorization

Clip Plan accesses Trello through Trello’s OAuth2 user consent flow. When you connect your account, the extension may use your authorized token to:

  • Retrieve your boards and lists
  • Create or update cards in the lists you choose

OAuth tokens are stored locally in your browser and are never transmitted to any Clip Plan-controlled server.

6. Revoking Trello Access

You can revoke Clip Plan’s access to your Trello account at any time.

  1. Go to your Trello account settings (e.g. Trello Account).
  2. Find the section labeled Connected Apps.
  3. Locate Clip Plan.
  4. Select Revoke Access.

After revocation, the extension will no longer be able to read or write any Trello data. You may also clear your browser’s extension storage or uninstall the extension to remove all locally stored data.

7. Children’s Privacy

Clip Plan is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Significant changes may also be highlighted in release notes or extension updates.
